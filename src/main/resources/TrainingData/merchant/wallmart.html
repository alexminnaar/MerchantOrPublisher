Nothing gets chocolate lovers cheering like a Chocolate Chip Quaker Chewy Bar. The kids will love each chewylicious bite with real, mouth-watering chocolate chips. Plus each bar has 8 grams of whole grains that you will love - it's no wonder they're the Official Snack of Back-to-School! Quaker Chewy Chocolate Chip Granola Bars contain 8 grams of whole grain and no high-fructose corn syrup, making these granola bars a tasty treat. The chewy granola and delicious, real chocolate chips offer a sweet snacking option.
