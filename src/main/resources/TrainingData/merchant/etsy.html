MRC Wood Products' personalized mason jar mugs make a wonderful gift for anyone. When you give these monogramed mason jar mugs, whether it be for newlyweds, bridal parties, anniversaries, or just because, the response will be fantastic. Each mug holds 16 ounces and measures 5 inches tall x 3 inches in diameter. The mugs will be engraved by sandblasting the image. Sandblasting provides a much deeper and richer engraving than laser etching.

SOLD INDIVIDUALLY

This design incorporates the first and last names of the couple and included the year the couple or family was established.

Mugs are sold individually so please select the quantity that you would like to purchase.

***HOW TO ORDER***
To order, please just click the "Add to Cart" button at the top of the page and proceed to the checkout. Please enter the information for the item in the notes section during the checkout. Please include:
1. The names and date to be on the item
2. Mugs come standard with the handle on the right. If you want it on the left, you must request it during checkout.

***SHIPPING***
Shipping - All shipping will be via UPS or USPS and most products will ship in approx. 8-10 business days from date of purchase. That may vary depending on the current workload we are experiencing. If you are in a hurry to get an item please let me know and I will do everything I can to speed up the process.

***BULK ORDER DISCOUNTS***
We offer discounts for orders of multiple items. The discounts are as follows:

6-11 glasses = 10% off the total. Use code 10OFF6GLASS

12 or more glasses = 15% off the total. Use code 15OFF12GLASS
