Designer

Former Cosmopolitan fashion editor Rebecca Hessel Cohen launched her imaginative label LOVESHACKFANCY in summer 2013 with the simple dream of designing the perfect, laid-back bridesmaid dress. Hessels original hand-dyed and printed silk dresses have since expanded into a wider collection of halter gowns, day-to-night dresses, and separates. As the brand continues to grow, LOVESHACKFANCY dresses stay true to Hessels vision of dancing barefoot in the garden under the moonlit summer night."
