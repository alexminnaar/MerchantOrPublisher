Product Information

Description

Waterproof your outdoor splices with the DryConn medium, aqua/red wire connector. This quality one-step connector is filled with a dielectric silicone sealant that surrounds splices, making it water and corrosion resistant. This is an ideal choice for outdoor electrical connections exposed to condensation, water vapors or dust. The DryConn aqua/red meets the conditions set by the most recent NEC code book, requiring a UL 486D listed connector to be used for all splices in wet and damp locations.

  • For use in patio and deck lighting, outdoor sound systems, exterior building lights, landscape lighting
  • Waterproof and corrosion proof
  • UL 486D listed, 600V rated
  • One-step installation with no pre-twisting of wires necessary
  • Minimum capacity of 2 #22 wires
  • Maximum capacity of 3 #8 wires
  • Made in the USA
