Product Overview

The Ryan Metal Bookcase flaunts an industrial design and a burnished black finish with open shelves and slim cross bars on its sides and back. The functional, open design holds books in place while letting you easily access them. This metal bookcase is perfect for your personal library, home office or a dorm room.

  • Open shelves with slim cross bars on sides and back
  • Burnished black finish
  • 5-shelf: 72 in. H x 36 in. W x 14 in. D
  • Assembly required
